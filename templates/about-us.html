About Us

Our History

Chandrajyoti English Boarding School Sindhuli was founded in 2072 BS by a group of educators who were passionate about providing high-quality education to students of all backgrounds. Our School has recently been improved, redesigned, reformed and restructured to provide better education to its students. With a committed administration and faculty, the school has been transformed into a state-of-the-art facility that offers a high-quality education to its students.

The transformation of Chandrajyoti School is expected to be a testament to the power of committed leadership and dedicated faculty. By investing in the facilities, curriculum, and faculty, the school has created a learning environment that promotes academic achievement and student success

Our Mission

The mission of Chandrajyoti School is to provide a challenging and engaging education that prepares students for success in a rapidly changing world. The school is dedicated to creating a supportive and inclusive community that values diversity, equity, and inclusion. The school aims to empower students to become confident, creative, and critical thinkers who are prepared to navigate complex global issues and contribute to a better future for all.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of our academic program and our community.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation and creativity in our approach to teaching and learning.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and foster a culture of inclusivity and respect.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity and ethical conduct.
